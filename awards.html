Best Thesis Awards

To recognize and celebrate outstanding research contributions, the conference will present Best Thesis Awards across three categories:

  • Undergraduate (UG) Level – Honoring exceptional research work by undergraduate students.

  • Postgraduate (PG) Level – Recognizing high-quality master's thesis research.

  • Doctoral (PhD) Level – Awarding excellence in doctoral research and its impact

The awards aim to encourage innovation, rigor, and excellence in research. To be eligible, the thesis must have been successfully defended between January 2022 and June 12, 2025.

Registration for the conference is mandatory to submit a thesis for consideration. Selected participants will be required to present their work at the conference, either in person or virtually.
