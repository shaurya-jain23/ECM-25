Best Thesis Award

Honoring Outstanding Contributions to Energy and Carbon Management Research

To recognize and celebrate outstanding contributions in the field of Energy and Carbon Management, the conference will present Best Thesis Awards across three categories:

Undergraduate (UG)

Honoring exceptional research work by undergraduate students

Postgraduate (PG)

Recognizing high-quality master's thesis research

Doctoral (PhD)

Awarding excellence in doctoral research and its impact

Eligibility & Submission

  • The thesis must have been successfully defended between January 2022 and June 12, 2025.

  • Conference registration is mandatory to submit a thesis for consideration.

  • Selected participants will be required to present their work at the conference, either in person or virtually.
