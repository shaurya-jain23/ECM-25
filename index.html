About Us

Energy and Carbon Management Conference: Case Studies, Progress & Future Directions, ECM 2025 brings together industry experts, researchers, policymakers, and energy professionals to discuss innovations and best practices in energy efficiency and carbon management. This platform will highlight real-world case studies, explore emerging technologies, and foster collaboration toward a sustainable future.

Aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), the conference will focus on practical solutions for industries, providing insights through plenary sessions, keynote lectures, expert panel discussions, and technical presentations.

Join us for an opportunity to gain valuable knowledge, network with industry leaders, and contribute to shaping the future of sustainable energy and carbon management.
