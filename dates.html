Call For Papers

The conference will cover the following topics, and we also welcome submissions on various aspects related to Energy and Carbon Management.

  • Industrial Energy Efficiency
  • Carbon Footprint Reduction
  • Renewable Energy in Industry
  • Energy Storage & Decarbonization
  • AI & IoT in Energy Management
  • Carbon Trading & Policies
  • Net Zero Energy Buildings
  • Decarbonizing Heavy Industries
  • Waste Heat Recovery
  • Sustainable Energy Roadmap
  • Energy in Transportation
  • Green Hydrogen in Industry

We invite participants to submit their research for the conference under two main categories:

Oral Presentations

Participants wishing to present orally must submit a full paper for review. The paper will be evaluated based on its quality, relevance, and clarity.

Guidelines for Full Paper Submission:

  • Word Limit: 3000-5000 words.
  • Formatting:
    Font: Times New Roman, size 12.
    Line Spacing: 1.5 lines.
    Margins: 1 inch (2.54 cm) on all sides.
    Page Numbers: Bottom center.
    Title Page: Include the title, authors' names, and affiliations.
    References: Use APA style for citations.
    File Format: PDF or Word Document.
  • Submission Deadline for Oral Papers: [insert date]

Poster Presentations

Participants wishing to present posters must submit an abstract of their work. The abstract will be evaluated based on its conciseness, clarity, and relevance.

Guidelines for Abstract Submission:

  • Word Limit: 300-500 words.
  • Formatting:
    Font: Arial, size 12.
    Line Spacing: Single spacing.
    Margins: 1 inch (2.54 cm) on all sides.
    File Format: PDF or Word Document.
  • Abstract Structure:
    Introduction: Brief overview of the research.
    Objectives: Clear research objectives.
    Methodology: Brief description of the research method.
    Key Results: Summarized findings.
    Conclusion: Main takeaways and relevance.
  • Submission Deadline for Abstracts: [insert date]

Proceedings: Abstracts of both oral and poster presentations will be included in the conference proceedings and will be assigned an ISBN and DOI.
Book Chapter: Selected oral papers will be published as a chapter in a Springer Nature Book (Proposal under review).
Registration: Registration for the conference is mandatory for all participants.
Presentation: Accepted participants will be required to present their work at the conference, either in-person or virtually.
