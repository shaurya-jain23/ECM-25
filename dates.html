We invite participants to submit their work for the conference under two main categories:

Oral Presentations

Participants wishing to present orally must submit a full paper or extended abstract for review. The work will be evaluated based on its quality, clarity and relevance.

  • The conference permits a Your Paper Your Way submission policy. However, authors are strongly encouraged to format their manuscripts using the provided template prior to submission.
  • Click here to download the template for submitting a full paper.
  • Click here to download the template for submitting extended abstract.
  • Submission Deadline for Oral Papers: May 15, 2025

Poster Presentations

Participants wishing to present posters must submit an abstract of their work. The abstract will be evaluated based on its conciseness, clarity, and relevance.

  • The conference permits a "Your Paper Your Way" submission policy. However, authors are strongly encouraged to format their manuscripts using the provided template prior to submission. Click here to download the template.
  • Click here to download the poster template.
  • Submission Deadline for Abstracts: May 15, 2025

Proceedings: Abstracts of both oral and poster presentations will be included in the conference proceedings and will be assigned an ISBN and DOI.

Book Chapter: Selected oral papers will be published as a chapter in a Springer Nature Book (Proposal under review).

Registration: Registration for the conference is mandatory for all participants.

Presentation: Accepted oral presenters must present their work at the conference, either in person or virtually. Poster presenters must attend in person.
